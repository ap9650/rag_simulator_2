Extra Important Step You Missed:

Most beginner RAG diagrams skip Embedding Generation, Vector Database Storage, and Prompt Augmentation. These are core parts that connect retrieval with the LLM.

1. Document Ingestion

1
Documents are uploaded into the system. This can include PDFs, FAQs, manuals, databases, webpages, or internal company knowledge.

2. Chunking

2
Large documents are split into smaller chunks. Chunking improves retrieval precision because embeddings work better on smaller semantic units.

3. Embedding Generation

3
Each chunk is converted into numerical vectors called embeddings. Similar meaning = nearby vectors.
